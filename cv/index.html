Abstract

While still pursuing my bachelor’s degree, I gained my first work experience being a Research Assistant at Fraunhofer IESE and was working there until I graduated with a master’s degree, 5 years later. After one and a half years of gaining experience at my first full time job, I had a rather radical switch at a fully remote, Switzerland-based startup. Being the first technical employee at the startup, I had substantial freedom in designing (from a technical perspective) and implementing the whole initial product. When the product gained more and more success, I was able to join my passions and figure out what it means to operate a SaaS product in a professional manor. After 4 years I decided to pick up a new challenge, working as a Cloud Engineer.

CLOUD ENGINEER @ RD, 2018 – now

In November 2018, I started working at a rather large company whose vision is innovating and leading the food retail digitization in Germany. I ended up in a 2-pizza platform/foundation team which is responsible for both operating a large fulfillment platform and providing cloud-native building blocks.

These building blocks

  • offer ready-to-use additions to the GKE/GCE platform that help solve common cloud problems such as monitoring, networking, transparency, secrets management, cost control, backups, etc.
  • are either self-written software, pre-configured sets of 3rd party software or both.
  • are used as a multiplicator for other internal teams

We typically try to follow the SRE approach of using max 50% of the time to operate the platform and the other time to automate toil away.
